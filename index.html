GamerCloud (Group Project)

In this project, we created a gaming based application. The user can enter a game title, on the left side of the page. This will then populate all of the available deals for that game, DLC packs, and more. A link to the deal is included in the results. On the right side of the page, a dropdown menu for gaming consoles can be selected to see giveaway items.
